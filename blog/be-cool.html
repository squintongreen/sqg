It's Hip to be Un-cool

September 26, 2013

115 words

There's a new four-letter word in town. It doesn't describe a vulgar action, it's not a slang euphemism for a body part, and it isn't a condemnation of any sort. It's a word that found its way into the English lexicon around the 12th century AD.

C-O-O-L.

I feel like we as designers have made it un-cool to do cool for cool's sake. Sometimes we simply want to do [ aesthetically pleasing ] things that will evoke a response something close to "Cool!"

And that's okay!

The cool factor should not be ignored. Do not be a slave to function. Have fun with form! And color! And composition! Be yourself - be bold!
