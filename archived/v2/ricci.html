Ricci International

Ricci International is a hair-studio and gallery located in Atlanta's historic Sweet Auburn district. Having already established a name in Jamaica, Ricci De Forest wanted to refine and refresh his brand for a new audience, without alienating his former clientele. I utilized the sharp angles and tight curves of his signature to connote a modernity that is active and progressive, yet balanced.
