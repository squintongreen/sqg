modern minimal glyphs

A study of simplicity and and modernist ideas in icon design, the modern minimal glyph family is a set of icons desinged for use on the web and mobile devices. The icons are built on a precise 2 pixel grid and are composed to accomodate a variety of screen sizes and resolutions, with little to no loss in clarity. The project was born partly out of neccessity, but also out of a desire to challenge myself by doing something I had never done before.
